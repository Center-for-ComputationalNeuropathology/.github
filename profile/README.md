## The Center for Artificial Intelligence & Computational Neuropathology

## About Us

Welcome to the Crary-Farrell Lab's Computational Pathology and AI repository. We are a dedicated research group at Mount Sinai and our mission is to harness the power of AI to advance our understanding of neurodegenerative diseases

## Get Involved

We welcome collaborations and contributions from the scientific community. If you're interested in our work or want to contribute, feel free to reach out or explore our repositories.
